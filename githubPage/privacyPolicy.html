Privacy Policy for Chat Plays OBS Twitch Extension

Last updated: 10/28/24

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Chat Plays OBS ("the Software") handles data collection and user privacy. By using the Software, you agree to the practices outlined in this policy.

Data Collection

The Software temporarily collects certain data to facilitate its functionality and provide an engaging user experience. The types of data collected include:

  • Click Locations: Information about where viewers click within the Software interface.
  • Opaque User IDs: A non-identifiable internal ID to associate actions with individual users without revealing their identity.
  • Twitch ID: Only collected if a Bit purchase or transaction occurs, for processing purposes.

Data Storage and Deletion

Local Storage: All data collected by the Software is stored locally on the streamer’s PC and is used exclusively to process interactions during the stream session.

Automatic Deletion: The collected data (logs) are automatically deleted when the Software is exited. No persistent or long-term storage occurs within the Software.

Data Usage

Interaction Processing: The collected data is used solely to facilitate viewer interactions and manage the Twitch stream layout based on viewer actions.

No Transmission: The Licensor does not transmit or collect any of the data for external use or analysis. All interactions and logs remain local to the streamer's machine.

Third-Party Services

The Software may connect to Twitch services via its API to authenticate viewers, process Bits purchases, or facilitate other interactions. The use of these services is subject to Twitch’s own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. Also cloudflares is used for data transmission and as such data is transmitted via cloudflare servers and as such by agreeing to this privacy policy you also agree to theirs https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/

Your Privacy Rights

As a user of Chat Plays OBS, you have the right to:

  • Access Transparency: Know exactly what data is being collected and for what purpose.
  • Data Anonymity: Rest assured that all collected data is non-personally identifiable unless tied to a Twitch Bits purchase.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in the Software or new legal requirements. Any significant updates will be announced within the Software or through other appropriate channels.
